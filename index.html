About Lesley

Lesley Tinnaro has always had an interest in art, but her passion for silver in 1995. Tired of a string of management positions, Lesley traded in her business suit for a hammer and anvil.

Lesley embraces the challenges and simplicity of the ancient tools of hammers, pliers, sweat and fire to create fine rings, necklaces, bracelets, and earrings by hand from sterling silver and semi-precious stones. Without mass production or casting each of Lesley's pieces are truly unique.

It is Lesley's wish that in wearing her work, you will share in the pride and joy that she receives when she creates it.

You can see Lesley's work in her Etsy showcase.
